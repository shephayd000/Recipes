Name: Buffalo Chicken Pasta
Ingredients:
    - 3-4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (2-3lbs), cut into 1-inch chunks
    - 2 10.5 oz cans condensed cream of chicken soup
    - 3/4 cup buffalo wing sauce
    - 1 medium red onion, finely chopped
    - 1/2 tsp salt
    - 1 tsp garlic powder
    - 2 cups sour cream
    - 1/2 cup ranch dressing
    - 1 cup mozzarella cheese
    - 1 lb penne pasta, cooked

Instructions:
    1. In a 5 quart slow cooker, mix the cream of chicken soup, wing sauce and onion.
    2. Season chicken chunks with salt, pepper (optional) and garlic powder.
    3. Add the chicken to the sauce and stir well.
    4. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours 
    5. Add in sour cream and ranch dressing.
    6. Stir in shredded mozzarella.
    7. Add cooked noodles to the buffalo chicken mixture in your slow cooker.
    8. Mix well.
Notes:
	-Didn't use onions, still tastes great
	-Set the crockpot on high, chicken was a bit
